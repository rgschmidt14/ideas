How it Works (The "Gee-Wiz" Info)

This tool fetches the current UV index for your location. The UV index is a measure of the strength of sunburn-producing ultraviolet (UV) radiation.

UV Index Scale:

  • 0-2 (Low): No protection needed. You can safely stay outside.
  • 3-5 (Moderate): Protection needed. Seek shade during late morning through mid-afternoon.
  • 6-7 (High): Protection essential. Wear a hat and sunglasses, use sunscreen.
  • 8-10 (Very High): Extra protection needed. Be careful outside, especially during peak sun hours.
  • 11+ (Extreme): Stay inside! Skin can burn in minutes.

Disclaimer: This tool provides an estimate for informational purposes only. Always use your best judgment and take appropriate precautions when outdoors.

Source: U.S. Environmental Protection Agency
